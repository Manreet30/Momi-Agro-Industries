Serving Excellence Since 2012

Since its inception in 2012, our cattle feed factory has been at the forefront of the animal nutrition industry, delivering high-quality feed solutions designed to enhance livestock health and productivity. Strategically located in Vill.Karhal Kalan,Dist.Kapurthala,Punjab, our state-of-the-art facility combines advanced manufacturing technologies with rigorous quality control processes, ensuring that every product meets the nutritional needs of various cattle breeds.

As we celebrate over a decade of operation, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering. We have earned the trust of farmers across Punajb, becoming a key player in the agricultural supply chain. Looking ahead, we are dedicated to expanding our product range, enhancing our production capabilities, and continuing to contribute to the success of the livestock industry.
